About Lisha Studio

A handmade art studio for playful objects, soft colors, gifts, candles, and little pieces that make everyday spaces feel warmer.

Hello, I’m Lisha.

Lisha Studio is where I create handmade objects inspired by small joys, cozy spaces, cute shapes, and everyday visual details.

The studio started with handmade candles, and it is slowly growing into a collection of art objects, decorative pieces, gifts, and playful little things.

Each piece is made in small batches, with attention to shape, color, texture, and the feeling it brings into a room.

What I make

I make handmade candles, fruit-inspired objects, cute character pieces, decorative home details, and experimental handmade art pieces.

Some pieces are functional, some are decorative, and some are simply made to be delightful.
